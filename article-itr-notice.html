ITR notice aa gaya? Ghabrao mat.

Notice ka matlab hamesha penalty nahi hota. Sahi samajh aur time par reply — bas yahi do cheezein chahiye.

By Neeraj Jain — Advocate · Founder & CEO, CGA India

Pehle ye samjho — notice kyun aata hai

Zyada tar notices routine hote hain: return mein koi mismatch (Form 26AS/AIS vs filed income), koi deduction ka proof, ya simple verification. Sirf kuch hi cases mein serious scrutiny hoti hai. Isliye notice dekh ke pehla kaam — section number padho. 143(1) intimation hai (mostly calculation match), 139(9) defective return hai, 143(2) scrutiny hai, 148 reassessment hai. Har ek ka matlab aur deadline alag hai.

Sabse important — time limit

Har notice mein reply ki deadline likhi hoti hai — usually 15 se 30 din. Deadline miss karna hi sabse badi galti hai, kyunki phir department one-sided order pass kar sakta hai. Agar time kam hai toh portal par adjournment request bhi daali ja sakti hai.

5 galtiyan jo log kar dete hain

  • Notice ignore karna — "galti se aa gaya hoga" soch ke chhod dena. Portal par pada notice legally served maana jata hai.
  • Bina documents ke jaldi mein reply karna — adhoora reply aksar naye questions khada karta hai.
  • Email/SMS check na karna — notices ab faceless hain, sab kuch portal aur email par aata hai. Registered email hamesha active rakho.
  • Galat ya banaye hue proofs dena — ye chhota issue ko bada case bana deta hai. Jo sach hai wahi file karo.
  • Professional ko late involve karna — pehla reply hi case ki direction tay karta hai. Shuruaat mein hi expert se draft karwana sasta padta hai.

Sahi process kya hai

Income tax portal par login karke e-Proceedings mein notice ka full text padho. Jo data department ne quote kiya hai (AIS/26AS) usse apne records se milao. Difference ka reason clear karo — documents ke saath. Reply portal par hi submit hota hai, acknowledgement save rakho. Agar demand galat lagti hai toh rectification ya appeal ka rasta bhi hota hai — deadline ke andar.

Kab professional ki zaroorat hai

Simple 143(1) mismatch aap khud bhi handle kar sakte ho. Lekin scrutiny (143(2)), reassessment (148), ya badi demand ho — toh representation experience matter karta hai, kyunki wahan drafting ke saath saath hearing bhi hoti hai. Hum CGA India mein filing se le kar appeal tak, poora matter ek hi desk par handle karte hain.
