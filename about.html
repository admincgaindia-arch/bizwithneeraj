The story behind Bizwith Neeraj

From a small-town practice desk to a firm serving clients PAN India.

Who I am

Practitioner by training. Builder by choice.

I built my practice across finance and law — because I kept watching clients fall into the gap between the two professions — the accountant prepares, the lawyer argues, and the client pays twice for half the clarity.

So I built a practice where both professions sit at one desk. Today, as Founder & CEO of CGA India — Canjain Global Advisors — I lead a team of 20+ professionals serving 100+ clients from Safidon (Haryana) and Burari (Delhi), available across PAN India.

Beyond the firm, I build PracEasy — software that modernises how CA firms run — and give time to skill-training work in my hometown as Secretary of Nav Bharat Sewa Sanstha.
