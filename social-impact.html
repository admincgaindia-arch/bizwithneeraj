Nav Bharat Sewa Sanstha

Skill training that changes what a family earns

Nav Bharat Sewa Sanstha is a charitable society based in Safidon, Haryana, focused on skill training. The belief behind it: certificates decorate walls, but skills change what a family earns.

As Secretary, I bring the same discipline to the society's work that I bring to client files — proper governance, clean compliance, and programmes measured by outcomes, not events.
