Main Safidon/Delhi se bahar hoon — kya aap mera kaam kar sakte hain?

Bilkul. ITR, GST, registrations — sab kaam online hota hai. Hum Haryana, Delhi, UP, Rajasthan, Punjab ke clients regularly serve karte hain — aur PAN India available hain. Documents WhatsApp/email par, baat call par.

Fees kitni lagegi?

Kaam ke hisaab se — ITR filing ₹499/- se shuru (offer price). Baaki services ki exact quote free 15-min call mein pehle hi bata dete hain. Kaam shuru hone ke baad koi hidden charge nahi.

Mujhe tax notice aaya hai — kya bahut bada problem hai?

Zyada tar notices routine hote hain — mismatch ya verification. Asli galti hoti hai deadline miss karna ya adhoora reply dena. Notice ki photo WhatsApp karo, hum section padh ke turant batayenge kitna serious hai aur kya karna hai.

Aap doosre consultants se alag kaise hain?

Do cheezein: (1) tax aur legal — dono ek hi desk par, matter kabhi transfer nahi hota, aur (2) main khud har critical file review karta hoon. 20+ ki team hai, lekin jawabdehi ek naam ki hai.

Naya business shuru kar raha hoon — pehla step kya hai?

Structure ka sahi decision — Pvt Ltd, LLP ya kuch aur. Ye decision din-1 par galat ho toh saalon bhugatna padta hai. Free call mein aapka business model samajh ke sahi structure batate hain — phir registration se compliance tak sab hum sambhalte hain.
